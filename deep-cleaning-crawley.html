Deep Cleaning Services in Crawley

Intensive, thorough cleaning that reaches every corner. Our professional deep cleaning service transforms homes and businesses, removing built-up grime and restoring freshness.

More Than Just a Surface Clean

Sometimes a regular clean just isn't enough. Whether it's months of accumulated grime, preparing for a special occasion, or simply wanting a fresh start, our deep cleaning service goes far beyond routine cleaning to deliver a truly transformative result.

Our deep cleaning tackles the areas that regular cleaning often misses – behind appliances, inside cupboards, on top of cabinets, inside ovens, and all those hard-to-reach places where dust and dirt accumulate over time.

When Do You Need a Deep Clean?

  • Spring cleaning – annual refresh for your home
  • Moving into a new property
  • Before or after hosting events or parties
  • Post-renovation or building work
  • After illness to sanitise your home
  • Preparing your home for sale or viewings
  • When regular cleaning hasn't been done for a while
  • Before starting a regular cleaning service

What's Included in a Deep Clean

Kitchen Deep Clean

  • Full oven clean including racks, door, and interior
  • Hob, extractor fan, and filters
  • Inside and outside of all cupboards and drawers
  • Behind and under appliances
  • Fridge and freezer interior
  • Microwave, dishwasher, and washing machine
  • Worktops, splashbacks, tiles, and grout
  • Sink and tap descaling

Bathroom Deep Clean

  • Limescale removal from all surfaces
  • Grout cleaning and mould treatment
  • Shower screen and enclosure descaling
  • Behind and around toilet
  • Tile cleaning floor to ceiling

Throughout the Property

  • Skirting boards, door frames, and doors

  • Inside windows and window frames
  • Radiators cleaned thoroughly
  • Cobwebs removed throughout
  • Detailed floor cleaning including edges and corners
